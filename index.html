About Us

Welcome to Sarthak's Cafe– a cozy corner where great coffee meets genuine connection. We’re passionate about crafting exceptional brews using our own self-roasted beans, carefully sourced and roasted in-house for peak freshness and flavor. Whether you're here for your morning espresso, an afternoon latte, or a quiet moment with a cold brew, we’re dedicated to making every cup feel like home. With a focus on quality, community, and comfort, we’re more than just a café – we’re your neighborhood’s favorite coffee spot.
